🔥 KidQueue Firebase Setup

⚠️ Required: Complete these steps to enable authentication in your KidQueue app

1. Enable Authentication Service

Enable Google sign-in for your app

🔐 Open Authentication Settings
  • Click "Get Started" if prompted
  • Go to "Sign-in method" tab
  • Click "Google" provider
  • Toggle "Enable"
  • Click "Save"

2. Enable Firestore Database

Set up the database to store user and queue data

🗄️ Open Firestore Settings
  • Click "Create database"
  • Select "Start in test mode"
  • Choose location: "us-central1"
  • Click "Done"

3. Verify Authorized Domains

Ensure your app domains are authorized

🌐 Open Domain Settings
  • Check that these domains are listed:
  • ✓ kidqueue-app.web.app
  • ✓ kidqueue-app.firebaseapp.com
  • ✓ localhost (for testing)

4. Test Your App

After completing steps 1-3, test the authentication

🚀 Open KidQueue App

Click "Continue with Google" - it should now work!

✅ Complete! Your KidQueue app authentication should now be working
